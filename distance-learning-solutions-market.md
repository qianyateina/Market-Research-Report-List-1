<p><p>Distance learning solutions can be categorized into two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based solutions offer flexible, scalable access to educational resources over the internet, enabling users to engage in learning from anywhere with an internet connection. This model typically requires a subscription and is managed by third-party providers. On-premise solutions, on the other hand, involve hosting software and resources within an organization's own infrastructure, offering more control and customization but requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance.</p></p>
