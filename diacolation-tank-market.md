<p><p>The Diacolation Tank market is categorized into two main types: Straight Tube Shape and Positive Cone Shape. The Straight Tube Shape tank features a uniform diameter throughout, offering straightforward fluid flow and easy maintenance, making it ideal for various industrial applications. In contrast, the Positive Cone Shape tank has a tapered bottom, enhancing the collection and discharge of sediments, which is beneficial in processes requiring efficient separation. Both designs are crucial for optimizing operational efficiency in different sectors.</p></p>
