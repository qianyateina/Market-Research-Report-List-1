<p><p>Handheld circular saws are available in two main market types: cordless and wired. Cordless circular saws offer the advantage of portability, allowing users to work in various locations without being tethered to a power source. They are powered by rechargeable batteries, making them ideal for outdoor or remote tasks. In contrast, wired circular saws provide continuous power, ensuring longer usage without the need for recharging. They are generally more powerful and suitable for heavy-duty tasks, making them a preferred choice for professional use.</p></p>
