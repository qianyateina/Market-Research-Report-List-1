<p><strong>The Card Personalization Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dye Sub</li><li>Retransfer</li><li>Inkjet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Card personalization services encompass three main market types: dye sublimation, retransfer, and inkjet printing. Dye sublimation involves transferring dye onto a card's surface, producing high-quality and durable images ideal for ID cards. Retransfer printing offers a protective layer by printing on a film before applying it to the card, ensuring vibrant colors and increased durability. Inkjet printing utilizes liquid inks, allowing for high-resolution graphics and flexibility, though typically less durable than the other methods. Each method serves various needs based on quality and cost.</p></p>
