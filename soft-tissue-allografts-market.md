<p><p>The soft tissue allografts market is categorized into three main types: hyaline, elastic, and fibro. Hyaline allografts are primarily used for cartilage repair, providing smooth, resilient surfaces. Elastic allografts, found in structures like ligaments and skin, offer flexibility and resilience, making them suitable for dynamic areas of the body. Fibro allografts, which are dense and fibrous, are essential for ligament and tendon repairs, offering strength and stability. Each type serves distinct clinical applications based on its unique properties.</p></p>
