<p><strong>The Branch Outlets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Threaded</li><li>Socket-Weld</li><li>Butt-Weld</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Branch outlet market types include threaded, socket-weld, and butt-weld connections, utilized in pipeline systems for branching off fluids. Threaded outlets feature screw-on fittings for easy assembly and disassembly, ideal for low-pressure applications. Socket-weld connections involve inserting a pipe into a socket fitting, promoting stronger joint integrity for high-pressure environments. Butt-weld outlets require welding two pipe ends together, ensuring a robust, permanent connection suitable for demanding conditions. Each type offers distinct advantages depending on application needs and installation requirements.</p></p>
