<p><p>The freight and logistics market is categorized into four primary types: airway, railway, roadway, and waterway. Airway transport is ideal for time-sensitive cargo, offering rapid delivery via air. Railway systems efficiently handle bulk goods over long distances at lower costs. Roadway logistics provide flexibility and accessibility for a wide range of freight, facilitating last-mile delivery. Waterway transport is cost-effective for heavy and oversized items, utilizing ships and barges to navigate vast distances, especially internationally. Each mode serves distinct logistical needs.</p></p>
