<p><p>The ultrasound linear probe market is segmented into three primary types: ultrasound straight probes, ultrasound angle probes, and ultrasound curvature probes. Straight probes are designed for high-resolution imaging in superficial tissues, often used in vascular and musculoskeletal assessments. Angle probes provide flexibility in accessing structures at varying angles, enhancing diagnostic capabilities, particularly in obstetrics and gynecology. Curvature probes feature a curved design, ideal for broader anatomical coverage, commonly utilized in abdominal imaging and cardiac evaluations. Each type serves specific clinical applications, optimizing patient care.</p></p>
