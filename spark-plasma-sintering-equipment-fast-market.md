<p><p>Spark Plasma Sintering Equipment, often referred to as FAST (Field Assisted Sintering Technology), is an advanced sintering technique that uses pulsed electric current to rapidly consolidate powders into solid materials. The process has gained traction across various industries, including ceramics, metals, and composites, due to its efficiency and ability to produce materials with unique characteristics.</p><p>The Spark Plasma Sintering Equipment (FAST) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.9% during the forecast period.
