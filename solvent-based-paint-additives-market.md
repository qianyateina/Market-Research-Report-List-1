<p><p>The solvent-based paint additives market comprises various types that enhance performance and application. Rheology modifiers improve the flow and viscosity for better application. Biocides protect against microbial growth, ensuring durability. Anti-foaming agents reduce surface bubbles, ensuring a smoother finish. Wetting and dispersion additives enhance pigment distribution and surface coverage, leading to a more uniform application. Other additives may include adhesion promoters, drying agents, and solvents, each tailored to improve specific aspects of paint performance and longevity.</p></p>
