<p><p>The Building and Construction Sealants market comprises several types, each serving specific applications. Silicone sealants are known for flexibility and weather resistance, making them ideal for glazing and sealing joints. Polyurethane sealants offer strong adhesion and durability, suitable for demanding environments. Plastisol sealants provide excellent adhesion to various substrates. Emulsion sealants are water-based, non-toxic options for indoor applications. Polysulfide sealants excel in chemical resistance, particularly in industrial settings. Butyl-based sealants offer strong moisture resistance, while other types include specialized formulations for niche uses.</p></p>
