<p><strong>The Metalworking Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Metalforming</li><li>Metalcutting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metalworking machine market encompasses two primary segments: metalforming and metalcutting. Metalforming involves processes that reshape metal using techniques like forging, bending, and stamping, which focus on altering the material's form without removing any material. In contrast, metalcutting involves removing material from a workpiece to achieve desired shapes and dimensions, utilizing tools such as lathes and milling machines. Both segments cater to diverse industries, including automotive, aerospace, and construction, driving innovation and efficiency in the manufacturing sector.</p></p>
