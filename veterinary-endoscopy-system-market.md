<p><p>The Veterinary Endoscopy System market is categorized primarily into flexible endoscopes, rigid endoscopes, and other endoscopes. Flexible endoscopes are designed for maneuvering through curved anatomical pathways, making them ideal for procedures in tight spaces, such as gastrointestinal or respiratory examinations. Rigid endoscopes offer better visualization for orthopedic and laparoscopic surgeries due to their stability and direct line of sight. Other endoscopes include specialized tools for specific applications, such as capsule endoscopes or ultrasonic endoscopes, catering to diverse veterinary needs.</p></p>
