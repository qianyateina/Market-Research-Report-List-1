DL-Malic Acid, a racemic mixture, is widely used in food, pharmaceuticals, and cosmetics due to its flavor-enhancing properties and acidity regulator functions. L-Malic Acid, the naturally occurring isomer, is favored in the food industry for its more intense tartness and is often utilized in health supplements. The demand for both types is driven by their applications in various sectors, including food and beverage, personal care, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
