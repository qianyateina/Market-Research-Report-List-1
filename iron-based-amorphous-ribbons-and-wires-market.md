<p><p>The Iron-based Amorphous Ribbons and Wires market encompasses two primary product types: amorphous ribbons and amorphous wires. Amorphous ribbons are thin, flat strips of iron with a disordered atomic structure, offering excellent magnetic properties and high permeability, used in transformers and inductors. Amorphous wires, on the other hand, are cylindrical and utilized in applications requiring efficient energy transfer, such as electrical coils and sensors. Both products are essential in modern electronics and energy applications, enhancing performance and reducing losses.</p></p>
