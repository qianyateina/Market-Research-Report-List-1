<p><p>The electric tile cutter market is primarily divided into two types: wet tile cutters and dry tile cutters. Wet tile cutters utilize water to cool the blade during cutting, reducing dust and heat, making them ideal for ceramic and porcelain tiles. Conversely, dry tile cutters operate without water, employing a high-speed diamond blade for cutting, which is convenient for quick jobs and on-site work. Each type caters to different preferences and job requirements in tile installation and renovation.</p></p>
