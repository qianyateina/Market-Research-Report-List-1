<p><ul><li>Open Frame Racks</li><li>Rack Enclosures</li><li>Wall-mount Racks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The data cabinet market encompasses three main types: open frame racks, rack enclosures, and wall-mount racks. Open frame racks provide a versatile and accessible design for housing IT equipment, promoting airflow and easy maintenance. Rack enclosures offer secure, enclosed spaces that protect sensitive hardware from environmental factors and unauthorized access. Wall-mount racks are compact solutions designed for limited spaces, allowing efficient use of vertical space while keeping equipment organized and easily accessible. Each type caters to specific needs in data management and storage.</p></p>
