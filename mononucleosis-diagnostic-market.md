The Epstein-Barr Virus (EBV) antibody test specifically measures antibodies against EBV, confirming the diagnosis of mononucleosis. Together, these testing methods provide healthcare providers with comprehensive tools to accurately diagnose and manage mononucleosis.</p></p>
