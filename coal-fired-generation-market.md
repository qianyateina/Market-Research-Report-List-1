<p><p>Coal-fired generation market consists of various technologies, primarily Pulverized Coal Combustion (PCC) and Cyclone Furnaces. PCC involves finely grinding and burning coal in suspension, achieving high efficiency and lower emissions, making it the dominant method for large power plants. Cyclone Furnaces, on the other hand, utilize a swirling motion to combust larger coal particles, allowing for greater fuel flexibility and reduced need for processing. Both technologies cater to different operational requirements and emissions standards in the energy sector.</p></p>
