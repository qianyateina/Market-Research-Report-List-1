<p><p>The Pigment Black market comprises various types, including Lamp Black, Acetylene Black, Gas Black, and others. Lamp Black is derived from the incomplete combustion of oils, offering high tint strength and excellent dispersion. Acetylene Black is produced from acetylene gas, known for its high electrical conductivity and purity, often used in specialty applications. Gas Black is manufactured from hydrocarbon gases, providing good pigment properties. Other variants may include furnace blacks and channel blacks, each tailored for specific industrial needs and applications.</p></p>
