<p><p>The metal forming lubricant market consists of four primary types: </p><p>1. **Water-based lubricants** use water as a primary carrier, offering environmental benefits and easy cleanup but may have lower performance in extreme conditions. </p><p>2. **Oil-based lubricants** provide excellent lubricity and are ideal for heavy-duty applications but can be more challenging to manage environmentally. </p><p>3. **Semisynthetic lubricants** blend both water and oil, offering a balance of performance and environmental friendliness. </p><p>4. **Synthetic lubricants** deliver superior performance in specific applications, though often at a higher cost, providing excellent thermal stability and lubrication properties.</p></p>
