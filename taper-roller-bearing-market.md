<p><p>The taper roller bearing market can be classified into single and multi types. Single taper roller bearings consist of an inner ring, outer ring, and tapered rollers, designed to handle axial and radial loads in a compact space. Multi taper roller bearings feature multiple rows of tapered rollers, providing enhanced load-carrying capacity and stability, ideal for heavy-duty applications.
