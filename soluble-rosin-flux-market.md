Liquid flux is versatile and commonly used for various applications due to its easy application and fast drying properties. Paste flux offers a thicker consistency, ideal for more controlled applications, providing excellent adhesion and coverage. Solid flux, typically in stick or block form, is favored for high-temperature processes, delivering robust performance and stability. Each type serves distinct needs in soldering and circuit board manufacturing.</p></p>
