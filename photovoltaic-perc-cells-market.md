<p><ul><li>Single-Sided PERC Cell</li><li>Double-Sided PERC Cell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photovoltaic PERC (Passivated Emitter Rear Cell) market comprises two main types: single-sided and double-sided PERC cells. Single-sided PERC cells capture sunlight from one side, enhancing efficiency through rear passivation. In contrast, double-sided PERC cells utilize both sides to collect sunlight, allowing for increased energy generation, especially in reflective environments. The choice between these types depends on installation conditions, cost considerations, and desired energy output, influencing their adoption in residential, commercial, and utility-scale solar projects.</p></p>
