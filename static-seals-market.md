<p><strong>The Static Seals Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial Static Seals</li><li>Radial Static Seals</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Static seals are crucial components in maintaining the integrity of equipment by preventing fluid leakage. The market is primarily divided into two types: axial static seals and radial static seals. Axial static seals function along the axis of components, providing sealing in bolted or flanged connections, commonly seen in hydraulic systems. In contrast, radial static seals operate radially around circular components, effectively sealing gaps in cylindrical applications like piping. Both types are essential in various industries, enhancing reliability and reducing maintenance costs.</p></p>
