<p><p>The glass mold market is categorized into three main types: Ordinary Cast Iron Mold, Alloy Cast Iron Mold, and Other Material Mold. Ordinary Cast Iron Molds are known for their cost-effectiveness and traditional use in glass manufacturing. Alloy Cast Iron Molds offer enhanced durability and resistance to thermal stress, making them suitable for high-precision applications. Other Material Molds encompass various alternatives, including steel and ceramics, which provide unique properties for specialized glass production needs, catering to diverse industry requirements.</p></p>
