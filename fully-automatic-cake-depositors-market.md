<p><p>The fully automatic cake depositors market is categorized into single-piston and multi-piston depositors. Single-piston depositors utilize one piston to dispense batter, making them ideal for simpler, lower-volume production needs. In contrast, multi-piston depositors employ multiple pistons to simultaneously dispense batter, allowing for higher efficiency and greater production capacity, suited for high-demand environments. Both types cater to varying requirements in cake production, providing businesses with options to optimize their manufacturing processes and meet consumer demand effectively.</p></p>
