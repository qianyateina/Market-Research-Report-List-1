<p><p>Marine navigation lights are essential for safe vessel operation, classified into two main market types: Basic Lightings and Lights of Special Significance. Basic Lightings include standard navigation lights such as sidelights, stern lights, and masthead lights, used to indicate a vessel's presence and movement. Lights of Special Significance, however, encompass unique signal lights like emergency markers and landmark beacons, which serve specific purposes in guiding vessels through hazardous areas or indicating critical navigation points in marine environments.</p></p>
