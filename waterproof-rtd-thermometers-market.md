<p><p>The waterproof RTD (Resistance Temperature Detector) thermometer market is segmented into single input and dual input types. Single input RTD thermometers measure temperature from one sensing point, making them suitable for straightforward applications. In contrast, dual input RTD thermometers can simultaneously measure temperatures from two different sources, allowing for more complex monitoring and control in industrial processes. Both types are valued for their accuracy, durability, and reliability in harsh, wet environments.</p></p>
