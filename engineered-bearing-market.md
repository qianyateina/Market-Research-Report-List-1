<p><p>The engineered bearing market encompasses various types, primarily including radial ball bearings and tapered roller bearings. Radial ball bearings are designed to support radial loads and are versatile for differing applications. Tapered roller bearings have an internal design allowing them to handle both radial and axial loads, making them suitable for heavier loads like in automotive applications. Additionally, the "others" segment includes specialized bearings such as needle bearings and angular contact bearings, catering to specific industrial needs and performance requirements.</p></p>
