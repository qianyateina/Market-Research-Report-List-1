<p><p>The Polymethyl Methacrylate (PMMA) sheet market is primarily divided into two types: extruded and cast. Extruded PMMA sheets are produced by forcing the material through a die, resulting in a continuous sheet that is typically more affordable and available in a variety of thicknesses. In contrast, cast PMMA sheets are made by pouring the liquid polymer into molds, allowing for higher clarity, thicker sheets, and more intricate shapes. Each type has distinct applications across industries, influencing their market demand.</p></p>
