<p><p>The UV disinfection equipment market consists of various technologies including Ordinary Cathode Low Pressure Mercury UV Disinfection, which utilizes low-pressure mercury lamps to emit UV-C light for effective pathogen inactivation. High-intensity Ultraviolet Disinfection employs high-output UV lamps to deliver stronger UV energy, enhancing disinfection efficiency. Ozone UV Disinfection combines UV light with ozone generation, providing both disinfection and oxidation capabilities, making it effective for water treatment. Each type offers distinct benefits tailored to specific application needs in water, air, and surface disinfection.</p></p>
