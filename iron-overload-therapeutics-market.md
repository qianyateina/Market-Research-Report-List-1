<p><p>The iron overload therapeutics market comprises three main types: Deferasirox, Deferoxamine, and Deferiprone. Deferasirox is an oral iron chelator that effectively reduces excess iron levels and is preferred for its convenience. Deferoxamine, administered via injection, is a traditional chelating agent used primarily in acute conditions. Deferiprone is another oral chelator known for its ability to target iron specifically in the heart. Each of these therapeutics plays a crucial role in managing iron overload disorders, enhancing patient quality of life.</p></p>
